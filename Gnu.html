-THE GNU-

(connochaestes)

The gnu is most commenly known as the wildebeest. The wildebeest is a very large antelope from Africa. The gnu is the largest of the antelopes. They are commenly related to goats, sheep, and cattle. The wildebeest's prefered habitat is in the savannahs and plains. They often eat grass, leaves, and shoots. Male wildebeests don't eat during the mating season.
