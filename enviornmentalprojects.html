ALL DOGS DESERVE TO BE RESPECTED

There are many dogs who are being abused and tortured while in incredibly awful homes. Puppy mills are considered as establishments that breed and sell dogs.
In some states puppy mills are not illegal beacause they provide food/water and shelter, but just because they provide it doesn't make it right, considering that the food/water and shelter isn't in the best conditions. The state with the most puppy mills is currently Missouri. Other states with high ratings of puppy mills are Ohio, Indiana, and pennsylvania.
